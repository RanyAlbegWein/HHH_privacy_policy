# Delete Your HHH Account

**App Name:** HHH
**Developer:** Rany Albeg Wein

## How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your HHH account and all associated data, please send an email to:

**Email:** [rany.albeg@gmail.com](mailto:rany.albeg@gmail.com?subject=HHH%20Account%20Deletion%20Request)

**Subject:** HHH Account Deletion Request

**Include in your email:**
- The email address associated with your HHH account
- Your nickname (if applicable)

## What Data Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, we will remove:

- Your profile information (name, email, profile photo)
- Your nickname
- Your subtitle/bio
- All jokes you have written (both published and pending)
- Your likes, dislikes, and favorites
- Your followers and following lists
- All activity associated with your account

## Data Retention

- Account deletion requests are processed within **30 days**
- Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered
- Anonymized analytics data may be retained for app improvement purposes

## Alternative: Sign Out

If you just want to stop using the app without deleting your data, you can simply sign out from the app's profile screen.
